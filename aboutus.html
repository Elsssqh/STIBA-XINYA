Our Journey

Our Journey

2018

Xinya College was officially established in Jakarta, Indonesia, as an innovative educational platform under STIBA Dagu University, pioneering the localization of liberal arts education.

2020

Launched its first interdisciplinary curriculum integrating philosophy, literature, social sciences, and digital humanities, earning recognition as an Innovative Education Project by the Indonesian Ministry of Education.

2022

Established partnerships with multiple international universities and launched the "Global Xinya Scholars Program," sending the first cohort of students overseas for joint research projects.

2024

Xinya College celebrated its inaugural graduating class, with 95% advancing to world-renowned universities or entering social innovation fields, significantly enhancing the college's reputation.
