Academic Exchange October 24, 2024

Global Cultural Exchange Program Launches with 20 Partner Universities

STIBA Tarumanagara Xinya is proud to announce the official commencement of our annual Global Cultural Exchange Program. This semester, we welcome over 150 students from 20 prestigious partner universities across Asia and beyond.

The program, designed to foster cross-cultural understanding and academic collaboration, offers visiting students a unique opportunity to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of Indonesian culture while pursuing rigorous academic coursework in language, business, and humanities.

A Bridge Between Cultures

"Education goes beyond textbooks," says Dr. Hendra Wijaya, Dean of STIBA Tarumanagara Xinya. "It is about building bridges between minds and hearts. This exchange program is a testament to our commitment to creating a truly global academic community where students learn as much from each other as they do from their professors."

"The diversity of perspectives in the classroom has been enriching. We are not just learning language; we are learning how to see the world through different eyes."

Throughout the semester, students will participate in a series of cultural workshops, including traditional batik making, Gamelan music sessions, and culinary excursions throughout Jakarta. These activities are designed to complement their classroom learning, providing a holistic educational experience.

Strengthening Academic Partnerships

This year's cohort includes students from our long-standing partners, including Fujian Normal University and Huaqiao University. The increasing number of participants highlights the strengthening ties between STIBA and international institutions, paving the way for future collaborative research projects and faculty exchange initiatives.

As the semester progresses, local STIBA students will act as "buddies" to the visiting scholars, ensuring they feel at home and helping them navigate campus life. This peer-to-peer mentorship is a cornerstone of the Xinya experience, fostering lifelong friendships that span across borders.
